Meet my Squishmallows

My very first Squishmallow I had gotten from Costco (unrelated but I love Costco) and his name is Ollie (short for Oliver) the Owl. He's very cute and soft and he hold's a special place in my heart because he was my first Squishmallow ever.

My second Squishmallow is also from Costco and his name is Draco the Dragon. He's got green scales down his back and a pointy tail. I remember I was between getting the dragon and an otter, but I asked my older brother who had accompanied me on the Costco run and he had sided with the dragon.

My third and last (for now) Squishmallow is a miniature one from Five Below. I am still mad that I didn't buy more at the time because when I had returned a few days later to look for Squishmallows to gift to my younger cousins, they had all been sold out. There was such a variety of mini Squishmallows to choose from and I picked an adorable sloth. His tag said his name was Simon and the tag description said his name was James so I introduce you to Simon James the Sloth.

I am on the hunt for more as I have become obsessed with my current ones. Every few weeks, I drive to approximately 5 different stores in my area that might have them to keep an eye out for new ones. I highly suggest everyone to get a Squishmallow at one point in their life since they are amazing. :)
